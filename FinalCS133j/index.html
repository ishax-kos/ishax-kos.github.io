How to play

You must slay Gurnok, for he plans to take the kingdom with his goblin horde!
Use the "A" and "D" keys to move left and right, and hold "S" to hit short foes.
Be carefull! Your shield only protects you where you aren't attacking! And you're always vulnerable from behind.
You can attack by pressing the "Right-shift" key and jump by holding "Enter".
You begin falling sooner when you let go of "Enter"
